About Us


Founded by Michael Chen and Daniel Martinez (a trained sushi chef and an expert in Vietnamese and Thailand food respectively), we bring the wonderful world of Asian cuisine right to your table. Home to Long Island, we aim to introduce more culinary options and further enrich this community’s culture. Ezo has deep connections to Japanese history, with our name paying homage to Hokkaido’s history and Chef Chen’s heritage. However, we also highlight the beauty of Vietnam and Thailand, to bring you a carefully-crafted fusion that maintains its authenticity.
